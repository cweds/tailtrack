TailTrack Privacy Policy

Your privacy and your pets' data protection

Effective Date: December 6, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to TailTrack, a comprehensive pet care management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application and web service.

TailTrack is designed to help families coordinate pet care activities across household members. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email Address: Required for account creation, login, and password reset functionality
  • Username: Your chosen display name within the application
  • Display Name: Optional additional name for household member identification
  • Password: Securely hashed using bcrypt encryption (we never store your actual password)

2.2 Household Information

  • Household Name: Custom name for your family unit
  • Household Invite Codes: Unique codes for family members to join your household
  • Membership Data: Information about which users belong to which households

2.3 Pet Information

  • Pet Names: Names you assign to your pets
  • Pet Types: Species information (dog, cat, fish, bird, hamster, rabbit)
  • Pet Photos: Optional images you upload (compressed and stored securely)

2.4 Activity Tracking Data

  • Care Activities: Records of feeding, bathroom breaks, walks, play time, grooming, medication, training, litter box cleaning, cage cleaning, and tank maintenance
  • Timestamps: When activities were recorded
  • User Attribution: Which household member performed each activity
  • Pet Assignments: Which pets were involved in each activity

2.5 Technical Information

  • Device Timezone: For accurate activity scheduling and care period calculations
  • Application Usage Data: Anonymous performance and error data to improve the app

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Functions

  • Provide pet care tracking and coordination services
  • Enable household member collaboration and activity sharing
  • Generate care status notifications and reminders
  • Maintain activity history and care analytics

3.2 Account Management

  • Create and maintain your user account
  • Authenticate your identity and secure access
  • Send password reset emails when requested
  • Provide customer support when needed

3.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze app performance and identify technical issues
  • Understand usage patterns to improve features
  • Ensure application security and prevent abuse

4. Information Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 Third-Party Services

TailTrack uses the following trusted third-party services to provide our functionality:

  • Supabase (Database): Securely stores all your account, pet, and activity data with enterprise-grade encryption
  • Resend (Email Service): Sends password reset emails only when you request them

4.2 No Data Sale or Marketing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your pet care data remains private to your household.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information only if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety and security of our users and the public.

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmitted and stored is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Password Protection: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with secure salt rounds
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure with regular security updates
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access systems

5.2 Data Protection

  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Comprehensive logging and monitoring for unauthorized access
  • Secure development practices with code review processes
  • Information disclosure protection to prevent data leaks

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access all your account and pet information through the app
  • Update Information: Modify your account details, pet information, and preferences
  • Delete Activities: Remove specific care activity records
  • Leave Household: Remove yourself from household sharing arrangements

6.2 Account Deletion

You can request complete account deletion by contacting us. This will permanently remove:

  • Your account and profile information
  • All pet data you created
  • All activity records you generated
  • Your association with any households

6.3 Data Export

Upon request, we can provide you with a copy of your personal data in a commonly used, machine-readable format.

7. Children's Privacy

TailTrack is designed for family use, including children with parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided personal information without permission, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and as required by law:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after
  • Deleted Accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Activity History: Maintained for household coordination; you can delete individual records anytime
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by applicable laws

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that your information receives adequate protection.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy
  • For significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by email
  • Your continued use of TailTrack after changes constitutes acceptance

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@tailtrack.app
  • Website: https://tailtrack.app
  • Response Time: We will respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days
